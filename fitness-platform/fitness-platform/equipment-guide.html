New to the Gym?

Beginner's Checklist

1️⃣

WARM UP

Always spend 5-10 minutes warming up. Treadmill, bike, or dynamic stretching.

2️⃣

START LIGHT

Start with lighter weights to learn the movement. Form > weight, always.

3️⃣

ASK FOR HELP

Don't be afraid to ask gym staff or experienced members how to use equipment.

4️⃣

REST & RECOVER

Muscles grow during rest. Get 7-9 hours sleep and allow recovery between sessions.
